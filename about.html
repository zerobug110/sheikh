About

Established in September 2008 with 7 pupils. The school is registered with the ministry of basic and secondary education (MOBSE). It has (2) levels i.e. nursery kindergarten and lower basic (primary /elementary. The ages are from 3-6 years for nursery and 7-13 years for primary. The total number of children in the school at this present moment is 291 children, 179 kids in the lower basic school and 112 in the nursery level and ten teachers.

Nursery Block Building project

The roof of the nursery block was destroyed by the wind during the raining season and it was renovated by Gambia bridge

The class partitions were broken woods and it was rebuilt with cement and new black board where bought. The whole building was supported with concrete pillars the make the building more strong. Then it was all repainted.

Mission

M

Create a favourable learning atmosphere for children to exercise their given talents and to develop their skills as they grow into full adults for the betterment of our beloved nation. We passion to give them a good foundation and to build them positively to help their dreams come to reality.

Vision

V

To transform the average child in our community into an equipped Godly and discipline youth with love for knowledge empowered with essential skills and self-confidence to contribute effectively to make the Gambia a great nation.
