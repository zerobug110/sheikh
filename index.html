Welcome Rhema Abam School

Welcome to Rhema Abam School, its a school situated in serrekunda london, Its a nursery and primary school.

Gambia Bridge Foundation

Gambia bridge is our main sponsors who are really doing well, they are sponsoring over 20 students for their school fees, books and uniforms

They are also helping us in our renovating classrooms and our library projects. Gambia bridge is not only sponsoring students within our school but in other schools too, these are students that have finished grade six in our school and moved to other schools.

About US

Established in September 2008 with 7 pupils. The school is registered with the ministry of basic and secondary education (MOBSE). It has (2) levels i.e. nursery /kindergarten and lower basic (primary /elementary. The ages are from 3-6 years for nursery and 7-13 years for primary.
